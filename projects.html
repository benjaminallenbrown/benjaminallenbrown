Power Launch

Work performed on behalf of Corinthian Events

Designed, sourced, and built a suite of digital marketing and event fundraising technology for Power Launch, a newly created nonprofit. Assisted in the planning and production of their inaugural event, "Pitch In", successfully launching the event to a packed audience and raised thousands of dollars for local non-profits.

Organization Website: Power Launch needed a website, but due to staff and budget constraints, they desired a solution that they could directly administer in the long term. I designed and built a website using Squarespace, and upon conmpletion, trained the Power Launch team to administer the system moving forward.

Email Marketing: Power Launch also needed a solution for email marketing to share information and build subscriber lists. After comparing options such as Constant Contact and Campaign Monitor, I selected MailChimp due to price, ease of use, and design flexibility using their no-code templates. I built out marketing automation and integrated it within Squarespace, set up subscriber lists, and built pre-defined templates for multiple purposes. Trained the Power Launch team to administer the solution moving forward.

Event Registration & Live Fundraising: Launched an integrated ticketing, voting, and fundraising system for "Pitch In" using 501 Auctions. Heavily customized the software to provide a consistent design between the organization website, registration platform, and movile application. Sent text messages, collected live donations and votes, and displayed a live leaderboard of results throughout the event program.
