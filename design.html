Mintz Levin (including ML Strategies & MintzEdge)

Work completed on behalf of Mintz Levin and included for portfolio purposes only.

Oversee the design and distribution of marketing emails, microsites, and registration forms for all internal events, reaching approximately 60,000 monthly users. Implemented design changes to improve user engagement informed by data analysis and A/B testing. Provide list and subscription management for mailings and CRM data.

Conducted Mintz Levin’s first statistical analysis of the entire company’s email marketing, encompassing almost 1 million email deliveries. Uncovered significant insights such as a 5% open rate increase in late afternoon and on Fridays, and identified a variety of design & process shortcomings. Implemented significant process and design improvements, along with A/B testing to examine new hypotheses based on the current data.
